Adoniram Judson

Adoniram Judson was the first American missionary to Burma. He and his wife Ann dedicated their lives to spreading the Gospel of Christ among the Burmese people. Despite facing numerous challenges, including imprisonment and torture, Judson never gave up on his God-given calling. His efforts were slow-going, but he persisted, and by the time of his death, he had established several churches in Burma and translated the Bible into the Burmese language. His work laid a strong foundation for Christianity in Burma and continues to inspire missionaries around the world.
